The purpose of my program is to have fun and complete all the levels in the game. In my game, you would have to click a crosshair that is placed at the middle of your screen every level and drag it over the “bad guy” to earn points. If you drag over a “good guy” you lose points. The input to my game is the click and dragging of the crosshair and the output of that would make the good or bad guy sprite disappear, give you points, and send you to the next level.
